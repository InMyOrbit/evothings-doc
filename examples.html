Kick-Start Example Apps

The EvoThings Example Apps are designed to get you started quickly with development of mobile apps for Internet of Things.

Included are both very basic apps that will work in any web browser, such as Hello World and Canvas Demo, and IoT-specific apps such as the Arduino examples and the Philips Hue Demo. The IoT apps require the EvoThings App to run.

Use these apps as a starting point for you own projects. Use them in your projects at work, play with them at home, try them with your college or university class, or use them with your kids at home! Spread the magic of IoT enabled apps!
