Meet the Evothings Client App

The Evothings Client app is the companion for the Evothings Workbench. This is a ready-to-use app, that will get you started instantly with building IoT-enabled mobile applications.

The Evothings client provides:

  • Automatic connection to Evothings Workbench.
  • Pre-packaged native libraries that extends JavaScript with advanced networking and connectivity for IoT.
  • Support for Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).
  • Automatic reload when you edit code on your desktop/laptop machine.
  • Full functionality of HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript, as provided by the platform of your phone or tablet's operating system.
  • Full compatibility with Apache Cordova plugins.
  • Source code provided to assist when building your production apps.

The Evothings client for Android is available on the Play Store, and on the download page.

The Evothings client for iOS is, as of April 3rd 2014, being reviewed for the Apple iOS App Store. The Xcode project is available on the download page.

Supported Plugins

The Evothings App comes pre-packaged with the following Apache Cordova Plugins:

  • org.apache.cordova.battery-status
  • org.apache.cordova.camera
  • org.apache.cordova.console
  • org.apache.cordova.device
  • org.apache.cordova.device-motion
  • org.apache.cordova.device-orientation
  • org.apache.cordova.dialogs
  • org.apache.cordova.geolocation
  • org.apache.cordova.globalization
  • org.apache.cordova.inappbrowser
  • org.apache.cordova.network-information
  • org.apache.cordova.vibration
  • org.chromium.socket
  • com.evothings.ble
  • org.apache.cordova.ibeacon
  • com.megster.cordova.bluetoothserial (included for Android only)
